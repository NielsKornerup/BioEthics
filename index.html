Eugenics


After finishing the AP Biology exam, our Biology teacher assigned us a project to learn about an ethical issue in biology. We (Niels Kornerup and Shawn Hingorani) decided to do our project on Eugenics, the attempt to genetically improve mankind. This project outlines the biology behind Eugenics, the ethical arguments for and against it, and some of its potential future implications.
